Chartboost

This is an advertising network. It needs to access various parts of your device including:

  • Your devices internet connection to be able to play advertisements.
  • Access the state of your devices network device to check if your device can access the internet.
  • Access the state of your WIFI to access the devices MAC address to identify you as a user.
  • Write to external storage to store advertising videos so they do not need to be streamed as they play.
  • Read your devices state so that it can close advertisements if you get a phone call.

By agreeing to this privacy policy you give Chartboost consent to process personal information for the purposes of advertising.
